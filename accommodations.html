Hotel

The hotels are located around the city and attractions, suitable for business and travel needs, with complete services and good value for money. Guesthouse

The guesthouse offers a localized lodging experience that gives you the opportunity to dive into the community and learn about the Tanitian culture. The guesthouses provide travelers with a warm and intimate atmosphere, usually run by local residents, at affordable prices and with a cultural flair. Here you will have the opportunity to immerse yourself in local life and feel the real pulse of Tanitian.

$89 / Night
